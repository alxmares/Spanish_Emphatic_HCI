Spanish End-to-End Empathic HCI

This study presents the first fully integrated empathic HCI pipeline in Spanish, combining ASR, multitask SER, protocol-driven reasoning via LLMs, and emotionally expressive TTS. Unlike previous systems constrained to isolated modules or non-Spanish languages, this framework enables proactive, affect-aware interaction across the full conversational cycle. Its modular and scalable design allows easy extension to other domains or tasks, facilitating continued development of emotionally intelligent systems.
